Description

Toasted bread or (Toast) for short, is a quick and easy snack anyone can make at home.

Below you will find our toast recipe.

Ingredients

  • Slice of bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter

    • Steps

  • Spread butter on slice of bread
  • Place bread in toaster
  • Toast until desired roast is reached
  • Enjoy!
