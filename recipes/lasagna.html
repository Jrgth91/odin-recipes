Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beautifully baked pasta dish that can be enjoyed anytime!

Below you will find one of our great recipes for said lasagna.

  • 1 pack of dry lasagna noodles
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 3 tbsp of grated parmasiano reggiano
  • 1/2 cup of shreaded mozzarella cheese
  • Italian spices

    • Steps

  • Lay amounts of sauce at bottom of baking dish
  • Place dry noodles on layers of sauce and cheese
  • Bake for some period of time.
