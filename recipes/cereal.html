Bowl Of Cereal

Description

Bowls of cereal have been around for thousands of years. Cavemen and aliens alike would partake in the morning ritual of enjoying a bowl of cereal!

A quick and enjoyful breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk or Milk substitute
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into bowl
  2. Pour milk over cereal in bowl
  3. Enjoy!
